FAQ
Questions answered.
How does the photo writing actually work?
You upload up to 3 property photos — kitchen, living room, backyard, whatever sells best. Listrolab analyzes each photo and identifies specific visual details: materials, finishes, architectural features, light quality, and condition. It then writes listing copy that mentions those specific details by name. A quartz waterfall island becomes "the waterfall quartz island catches the afternoon light." French doors become "French doors open to a mature garden." You also see a panel showing every detail spotted from your photos.
Do I need to be a good writer to use Listrolab?
No. You just need to fill in a form. Listrolab handles the writing. The more detail you give about the property and target buyer, the better the output — but even basic inputs produce solid, usable listing copy.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
You could use ChatGPT the same way you could cut your own hair — technically possible, takes forever, and the result shows it. To get a decent listing from ChatGPT you'd spend 20 minutes writing a detailed prompt explaining MLS conventions, tone, word count, structure, buyer type, and emotional hook. Then you'd read the output, realize it's generic, rewrite the prompt, try again. Every single listing, from scratch. Listrolab replaces that entire process with a 90-second form and a generate button. It already knows MLS structure, buyer psychology, and how to write copy that actually sells. The photos feature alone is something ChatGPT can't replicate in any practical way — upload 3 photos and Listrolab writes from what it literally sees, mentioning your specific finishes, materials, and details by name. No prompt engineering required. Ever.
Is my property data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit with 256-bit SSL. We do not sell your data to any third party. Your listing data is stored on US-based servers. You can request deletion of all your data at any time. We use the same security standards as enterprise SaaS companies.
Can I edit the generated listing?
Absolutely. The output is a starting point, not a final product. Most agents copy it, make minor tweaks to match their voice, and paste it straight into MLS. You can also regenerate with different tones until you find the one that fits.
What is buyer framing?
Buyer framing automatically rewrites your listing three times — once targeted at young families (schools, space, community), once for investors (rental yield, location fundamentals, appreciation), and once for downsizers (low maintenance, quality finishes, walkability). Same property facts, three different emotional hooks. Pro feature.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings with no questions asked. For annual plans, you'll keep access until the end of your billing period. We don't do cancellation run-arounds.
